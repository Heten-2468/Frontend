My Top 3 Movies of all time...

Old Boy

This movie is the greatest piece of dark fiction I have ever seen.
This movie fucked me up and not in a bad way. I was traumatized and amazed.

A Silent Voice

This movie made me cry, smile and I related to it like I had just found a memory in a different shade.
This movie is one of the most recommended movie for any age group above 10.

Gangs of Wasseypur

This dark gritty tale of rural U.P. was just the holy grail for my teenage mind that I was looking.
Why this was so great, see and then you tell me...
